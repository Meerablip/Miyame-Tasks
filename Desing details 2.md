## The directors Page: 

1. For the Director, the sign-up and login will remain the same, nothing changes, once a user sign’s up with using the role director the application inside will have the changes. 

2. The application will open to a page that will have list of employees who have the application. 

   - i. On top center of the page will be current date, below that will be the list of employees, and each will be openable to that employee’s to-do list for that day. 

   - ii. The director can comment on any task; all the tasks will be clickable, on clicking a task, from the bottom of the page will come the details of the task that the employee has put, and there will be a section for comment there, the director can write and save a comment. 

   - iii. This comment will trigger a push notification for the employee: “Comment on the task ” or something similar. 

   - iv. Other than that, the director can create a task for the employee, and put a schedule to it, he cannot set an alarm tho. 

   - v. It will work this way: the director will click on the employee, take a look at his task and to-do list and the status of completion, if something is complete it will turn greyish, the director won’t be able to make changes to that, and there will be a button called “assign task” at the bottom of the screen. 

   - vi. The director will click this button and fill the details that are mentioned in the 1[st] design doc, and press a button called “assign task”, this will work exactly how it does for employee’s create task. 

   - vii. Once a director assigns a task to the employee, it will trigger a push notification for that employee, and the task assigned will have say light orange color to the whole bar, so that it can be differentiated. And the assigned task will always remain on top. 

   - viii. At the end of the day or at 22:00pm every day the directors will receive a notification to check the progress, just that one, nothing else. 

   - ix. In the desktop this employee list will look a little different it will be like it does for boards In Trello, all the task list of the employee will be horizontal and the Dir can scroll side ways to take a look at it, also, there will be an option to change the view in the top right corner, for the desktop, to an employee list, and on top will be a search bar that will allow them to search. 

   - x. Everyday the list of the tasks of employees will change to the current date and their current date’s tasks that they assign. 

   - xi. In the top right corner of the phone and the desktop (beside the view button) will be a filter button which will have filter with category and site. 

   - xii. And just like the employees there will be a hamburger nav menu in the top left corner which will slide in from the left side. 

3. The hamburger menu will have the following things leading to separate pages: Calendar, To-do, Create site, Category, profile and Reports. 

4. Calendar: The calendar page will be exactly like the one for the employee, the today’s date will be highlighted using shadows of the border, without any fancy colour, on top will be the Current month, year and the < and > to navigate, the year and month can be changed by clicking them, and cho0sing from the drop down list. 

   - i. Every date in the future, even a day after today’s date, that has a task assigned by any employee to will have a blue shade at the top, even if only one employee assigns a task for the future, the date will have a blue line there, the director will can understand the assignment of future task by: > this will turn blue in the right side, and will be until director navigates to the calendar that has the new task assigned. 

   - ii. Every date in the calandar will be clickable, it will pop two questions on clicking: 1. Create a To-do and 2. Assign a task. 

   - iii. If the director clicks create a to-do he will be taken to a page where he can create a to-do list for himself and schedule it, exactly like creating task for employee is. If he does so the shade on the top border of that date will turn pink. 

   - iv. If the director clicks Assign a task, he will be taken to a page which will have the date and all on top and a search bar and an employee’s list, he can click on the employee that he want’s and that will take him to another page that will have the date on top, hamburger menu on the top left corner and will have a long button saying “assign task” this will have the details, like description, category, schedule time, and a comment section to write a comment. 

   - v. On assigning the task to an employee the employee will get the notification, saying task assigned for the date. And the date there. 

   - vi. That will be it for calendar. 

5. Next in the hamburger menu will be the To-Do, A director will have a to-do creation option for every day, the to-do will only open on the current date and exactly will function the way the employee’s to-do list will work, he can add task, put it to priority, it will change everyday to the current date, old tasks will be shifted to new one, all the tasks will have the details and all the same. 

6. If the Dir creates a to-do on his Calander the green dot and the yellow dot function will be there just the way it is for the employees. 

7. Next in the hamburger menu will be the create site option, now the Director needs to create site to allow his employees to pick site from drop down and for him to pick site while assigning tasks. 

   - i. This page will have a single button called create site, and on clicking that there will be a pop up to fill the Site name that will be in the drop down of the add site when the employees and director create and assign tasks. 

8. Then will be category, it will be exactly like it is for the employees, nothing different. 

9. Then will be a profile, That will remain the same for the director as the employee as well with the light and dark theme option, light being default. 

10. After then will be the reports in the hamburger menu, the reports will be similar to the the employees' there will be two sections there, category and site, and when the dir open category there will be list of category, on clicking any list there will be a list of employees with the statistic number of tasks that they did beside their names, on opening any employee, will be a date wise sorted list of the tasks they did under that category and a date filter, in the top right corner, and the same list of site within each site list of employees who worked on the site, and list of sorted task inside each employee, will be present.

11. A few other things, In the employee, above the calendar in the hamburger menu add another thing called To-do that will be the default home page, and will have the everyday to-do list thing Just link it to the hamburger menu. 

That’s it for the complete application.
